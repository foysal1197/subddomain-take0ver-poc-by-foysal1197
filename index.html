Problems

Creating Pipeline is Harder Than Ever

Technical buyers don’t want to talk to salespeople.

CTOs, CISOs, CIOs, CFOs and other buyers increasingly resist engaging with salespeople. This credibility gap makes it extremely hard for SDRs and AEs to penetrate accounts and generate pipeline effectively.

Email sequences have a
0% success rate.

The average SDR runs 10-16 steps in a sequence, but when it doesn’t land a meeting, it ends there. Why stop when you have an entire team of technical experts, senior leaders, and executives who can break through?

Involving non-SDRs is nearly impossible.

Senior leaders, technical experts, and subject matter specialists get substantially higher reply rates from prospects. But, involving them is a logistical nightmare. Drafting messages, providing context, and managing approvals creates friction that prevents this GTM motion from scaling effectively.

Unlock company-wide outreach

Yess empowers your SDRs and AEs to initiate email and LinkedIn outreach from any employee within your company including technical, senior staff, subject matter experts, and C-level executives with a click of a button.

Find the best path to the prospect

Yess’ proprietary AI finds the ideal team member to engage prospects by mapping out and leveraging proximity to prospects, job titles, mutual connections, previous interactions and their willingness to engage with accounts with specific criteria.This helps your team find the best path to their prospects.

Turning “dead” leads to new opportunities

Sequences sent from SDRs and AEs have on average a 2% positive reply rate. Moving 98% of leads to marketing nurture campaigns overlooks high-potential prospects that are likely to answer to outreach from technical, senior staff, subject matter experts, and C-level executives. Supercharge your Outreach or Salesloft sequences by adding automatic Linkedin and email steps conducted by any employee of your company (or start such a sequence directly from Yess).

Maintain controlled and secured team-wide outreach.

Yess's Approval Workflow feature offers flexible approval options for technical, senior staff, subject matter experts, and C-level executives. From manual reviews to automated rules, and integrates seamlessly with Slack. With enterprise-grade customizable approval hierarchies (by person, SFDC segmentation and more) each person in the company can control when SDRs and AEs can ask them to reach out to customers and how many times a week.

Multi-channel sequencer

Build many to many multichannel (LinkedIn + Email) sequences. Our Email and enterprise–grade LinkedIn Sequencer allows each team member in your company to to automate tasks such as: LinkedIn connect, LinkedIn message, Profile view, Likes, and more. Enable your SDR and AE teams to initiate sequences from any person within your company.

Make the SDR’s work more efficient, strategic, and impactful.

Yess’ AI assistant recommends optimal next steps, identifies the most suitable team members for outreach, crafts personalized and contextually relevant messages, and prioritizes leads based on real-time signals. You can also use our AI autopilot to automate outbound for tier-3 accounts.

Set a new standard in lead prioritization and engagement

By integrating hundreds of data points from both first-party and third-party sources, Yess creates a comprehensive view of each lead's intent and potential, ensuring that your team engages the right leads with personalized approaches at the optimal moment, significantly enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of your sales efforts.
